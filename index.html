Homemade Lasagna

This is a recipe for a special homemade lasagna recipe with a meaty, made from scratch tomato sauce, with a nice cheesy filling. A more Traditional way to make lasagna would be made with ricotta however this will be a blend of small curd cottage cheese and Parmesan.

I have not, as of yet, found a recipe that would even remotely top this (I mean it). Ideally to be served with a leafy green salad and crusty garlic bread or any of your favorite sides.
