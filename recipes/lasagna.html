Homemade Lasagna recipe

For this Piping hot dish recipe, we will need the following ingredients;

  • ½ pound ground pork
  • ½ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 1 can crushed tomatos
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 ½ tsp dried basil
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • 1 package lasagna pasta
  • 1 pound of cottage cheese
  • 3 Large eggs
  • 2 tsp ground black pepper
